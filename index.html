Food labels are written for compliance, not clarity. Consumers are left to interpret chemistry and trade-offs under time pressure.

Conflicting or unclear health guidance
High cognitive load at decision time

About Our Technology

Health-Buddy is an AI-native food label co-pilot built to help users understand what they are consuming at the exact moment of decision.

Core Technologies

GPT-4.1 Mini

Reasoning and explanation layer

GPT-4V (Vision)

Label text extraction

OpenAI TTS

Natural audio responses

Key Features

Real-time label scanning
AI-native explanations
Session-based context
Voice and text interaction
Privacy-first design

How the System Works

Scan food label
Extract visible text once
Create temporary session
Generate explanation
Answer follow-ups in context
Discard session data

Technical Specifications

Frontend: React (Web)
Backend: Node.js & Express
Hosting: GitHub Pages & Render
Security: HTTPS-only
Performance: Near real-time
